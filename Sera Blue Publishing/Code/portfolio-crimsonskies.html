Crimson Skies

It does not rain. Not unless someone is killed. It’s been four months and the soil is hard and dry. People are suffering, the dam is low.

Sarla, Dyle, Jex and Aila are the next chosen offerings. They will be sacrificed for rain, that is, if they don’t escape first. The escape through the canyon is full of dangers, but stars rise on the other side.

Surely it is safe where stars deem it fit to shine?
